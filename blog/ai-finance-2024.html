AI Hits Wall Street: How GPT-4 Is Powering the Next Wave of Fintech

June 5, 2024 • 5 min read

Fintech startups are racing to plug GPT‑4 into every step of the investment pipeline. From scraping filings to drafting portfolio memos, large language models are doing work that used to take analysts days.

Real-time research. Funds are using GPT‑4 to parse earnings calls as they happen, surfacing sentiment shifts before the transcript even lands.

Hyper-personalized banking. Chatbots built on top of customer data now recommend savings goals, flag suspicious transfers and automate invoices for freelancers.

Smarter risk models. By combining structured market data with unstructured news feeds, GPT‑4 spots correlations humans miss, helping traders hedge faster.

The upside is massive, but so is the responsibility. Bias, security and compliance are still open questions—and the firms that solve them first will own the next decade of finance.
