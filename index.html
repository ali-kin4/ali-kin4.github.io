About Ali

Ali Jabbary, M.Sc., P.Eng., is an internationally recognised tutor and consultant trusted by Fortune 500 teams and 500+ learners worldwide. With dual degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Data Science, Ali has spent more than a decade delivering data‑driven solutions, coaching and mentoring students across Iran and Canada. His mission is simple: transform ambitious students into high‑impact professionals through tailored, results‑oriented instruction.

Ali’s lessons are engaging, hands‑on and customised to each student’s goals. Whether you’re mastering calculus, building machine learning models or optimising engineering designs, Ali guides you every step of the way with clarity, patience and real‑world examples.
